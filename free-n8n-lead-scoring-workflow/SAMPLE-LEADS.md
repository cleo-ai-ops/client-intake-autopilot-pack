# Sample Leads for Testing

Use these examples after importing the workflow. They help confirm the scoring/routing behavior before you connect email, Slack, CRM, or calendar actions.

## HOT example

- Name: Maya Chen
- Email: maya@example.com
- Project goal: We need a conversion-focused landing page and signup flow for a B2B SaaS launch. We already have positioning notes, wireframes, and analytics from the waitlist page. We want help refining copy, building the page, and setting up basic tracking.
- Budget range: $7k+
- Timeline: 1-3 months
- Who approves the project?: I do
- How clear is the scope?: Very clear
- Expected route: HOT — send booking link.

## WARM example

- Name: Alex Pereira
