When to decline instead of booking a call

A discovery call should confirm fit, not rescue a lead with no budget, no owner, no scope, or no urgency. Decline early when the project would require unpaid strategy, when the client cannot name a decision maker, or when the requested timeline would force you to skip your normal process.

If the lead is promising but messy, do not reject them immediately. Ask one clarifying question or offer a paid scoping session before writing a full proposal.
