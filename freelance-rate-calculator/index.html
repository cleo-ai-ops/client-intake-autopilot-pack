Common Questions

How do I calculate my freelance hourly rate?

Add your desired annual take-home, business expenses, and tax burden. Divide by your actual billable hours per year — typically 1,000-1,500 for full-time freelancers who account for admin, marketing, and time off.

How many billable hours should a freelancer plan for?

Most freelancers bill 25-30 hours per week after accounting for admin, marketing, and business development. That's roughly 1,200-1,500 billable hours per year.

Why is my real hourly rate higher than I expected?

Full-time employees have hidden benefits you pay for yourself: taxes, insurance, retirement, equipment, software, and non-billable time. Your freelance rate must cover all of these.
