Routing rules

  • Hot, 9–12: send a booking link, plus one detail from the intake answer.
  • Warm, 6–8: ask one clarifying question before offering a call.
  • Cold, 0–5: decline politely, send a resource, or offer a paid audit.
  • Risk flag: set boundaries before any call, even if the numeric score is high.
