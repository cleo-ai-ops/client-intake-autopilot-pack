  9. What budget range is approved?

Turn answers into a decision

The form only helps if every answer maps to a next step. Use a simple rule: strong-fit leads get a booking link, unclear leads get one clarifying question, and weak-fit leads get a polite decline or referral.

Fast scoring rule:
  • 2 points for clear business outcome.
  • 2 points for approved budget range.
  • 1 point for realistic timeline.
  • 1 point for decision maker access.
  • Subtract 2 points for vague scope or “just exploring”.
