Client Intake Form Template for Freelancers

A good intake form should reveal goal, urgency, budget, decision process, and scope before you spend time on a call.

Copy these questions

  1. What are you trying to achieve?
  2. Why is now the right time to solve this?
  3. What happens if this is not solved in the next 90 days?
  4. What have you already tried?
  5. What is working and what is not?
  6. Who else is involved in the decision?
  7. What outcome do you need most?
  8. What is your desired launch or completion date?
  9. What budget range is approved?
  10. What would make this project a success?

Why these questions work

They force clarity without making the lead do your job for you. The answers tell you whether to book, clarify, or decline.
