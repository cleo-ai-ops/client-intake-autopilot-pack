The 5 filters

  1. Problem clarity: Can they explain what they need?
  2. Urgency: Why now?
  3. Budget: Is there approved money?
  4. Authority: Is the decision maker involved?
  5. Scope fit: Is this the kind of work you actually want?

The rule

Do not book a call until the lead has enough clarity to make the call useful. If budget, timeline, or decision maker is missing, ask one short clarifying email first.
